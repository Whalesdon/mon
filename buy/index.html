The most efficient way to buy millions of dollars of PLS is on app.PulseX.com. You need a few dollars of PLS to pay for gas to use it. You can buy ETH, DAI, USDC, USDT or WBTC on Ethereum from somewhere, then bridge.pulsechain.com them to PulseChain, where you can swap for PLS with massive liquidity on PulseX. At the time of writing DAI is the best token to bridge over for buying PLS, because it has the most liquidity.

The less liquid options above are nice because they support lots of blockchains and OKX even supports normal money payments for many outside the united states.
